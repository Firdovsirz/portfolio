### Engineering approach

- **System design first.** Data models that survive organisational change; bounded contexts; explicit service boundaries.
- **Scalable architectures.** Modular monoliths with clear seams, event-sourced pipelines where audit matters, read-optimised projections where reporting matters.
- **Backend engineering.** PostgreSQL-first, REST and WebSocket APIs, role-based authorization without permission sprawl.
- **API integrations.** Reconciliation layers across third-party academic indices with conflicting identifiers, rate limits, and partial outages.
- **Distributed systems interests.** Consistency and coordination across services, regions, and organisations.
